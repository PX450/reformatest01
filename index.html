About Reforma Trust

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged youth through comprehensive education, mentorship programs, and community development initiatives that create lasting positive change.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of educators and community leaders, Reforma Trust has grown from a single classroom initiative to a network of 12 community centers serving over 5,000 children annually.

Core Values

  • Compassion: We meet every child where they are with empathy
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in all our programs
  • Community: We believe in the power of collective action
